About Us

Student Result Management System is a very Friendly System which helps the teachers and the students to maintain a easy way of tracking the marks and keep updating. The system is very well maintained.



The Students can come and see their marks after entering their Enrollment Numbers. The Total Marks along with the average of each students is displayed.



The Teachers have their registered Mail Id given by the Institute. The Teachers can log in and enter the students marks of all the subjects.
